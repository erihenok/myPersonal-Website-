ABOUT

Henok found his passion for photography at a young age when he inherited his grandfather’s 35mm camera. he knew from the moment he took his first picture that this was his life’s path. This passion for seeing the world through imagery took his to Asmara Institute of Photography where he continued to expand the foundation on which his future photography career is based. Shortly after graduation, he went to Eritrean national service to serve his country.He served his country as a photo and videographer. Having specialized in weddings for more than 10 years, he continues to love photographing both weddings and families and looks forward to being part of these cherished moments in your life.
